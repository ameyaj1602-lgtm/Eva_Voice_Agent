# Eva - Your Personal Voice Companion

## What is Eva?

Eva is an AI-powered voice companion that adapts to your emotional state. She listens, talks, motivates, calms, and supports you — like having a best friend, therapist, and coach available 24/7 in your pocket.

Everything you share with Eva stays private. Think of it as your room — a safe space where you can be completely yourself. No judgments. Just you and Eva.

---

## What Can Eva Do?

### Talk to You in 9+ Mood Modes

Eva shifts her entire personality based on what you need right now:

| Mode | What Eva Does |
|------|--------------|
| **Calm** | Soothes you with gentle words, guides breathing exercises, helps you find peace |
| **Motivation** | Fires you up like a coach. Direct, powerful, no excuses. Gets you moving |
| **Charming** | Warm, flirty, playful. Makes you feel special and desired |
| **Therapist** | Empathetic listener. Asks thoughtful questions, validates your feelings |
| **Companion** | Your loving best friend. Laughs with you, checks on your day |
| **Lullaby** | Whispers bedtime stories, sings you to sleep with a dreamy voice |
| **Storyteller** | Tells epic tales, creates immersive narratives just for you |
| **Comedian** | Makes you laugh with witty jokes, observations, and perfect timing |
| **Philosopher** | Explores deep questions about life, meaning, and consciousness |
| **Custom** | Create your OWN mode with any personality you imagine |

### Voice-First Experience

- **Speak to Eva** — tap the mic, talk naturally, she understands you
- **Eva speaks back** — with realistic AI voices that change per mood
- **Voice cloning** — capture anyone's voice (a loved one, a friend) and Eva speaks in their voice

### Smart Emotion Detection

Eva reads between the lines. When you say "I'm so stressed," she suggests switching to Calm mode. When you say "I can't sleep," she nudges you toward Lullaby. She adapts to YOU.

### Wellness Tools Built In

| Tool | What It Does |
|------|-------------|
| **Breathing Exercise** | 3 patterns: 4-4-6 Calming, 4-4-4 Box, 4-7-8 Sleep |
| **Meditation Timer** | 5, 10, 15, 20 minute sessions with a bell sound |
| **Sleep Timer** | Set Eva to stop after 15-60 minutes |
| **Pomodoro Timer** | 25 min focus + 5 min break cycles for productivity |
| **Mood Tracker** | Log your mood daily, see your 7-day emotional chart |
| **Gratitude Journal** | Daily prompt: "3 things you're grateful for" |
| **Streak Tracker** | See how many consecutive days you've talked to Eva |

### Conversation Starters

Don't know what to say? Eva gives you 3 thoughtful prompts based on your current mode. Just tap one and start talking.

### Daily Affirmation

Every time you open Eva, a fresh affirmation greets you — a small reminder that you matter.

### Your Data, Your Privacy

- All conversations stored on YOUR device
- No one sees your chats except you
- Eva remembers things about you (your name, preferences, favorites) to make conversations more personal
- You can export your chat history anytime

### Works Everywhere

- Works on phone, tablet, laptop — any browser
- Install it like an app on your home screen (PWA)
- Runs 24/7 — available whenever you need her

## How Eva's Brain Works

Eva uses multiple AI systems with automatic failover:

1. **Google Gemini** (primary) — fast, conversational
2. **OpenAI GPT-4o-mini** (backup) — if Gemini is unavailable
3. **Offline responses** (fallback) — pre-written responses if all APIs are down

She never crashes. If one brain fails, the next one kicks in instantly.
